Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

14-Day Return Policy

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery if items are in original condition.

Eligibility

  • Items unused and in original packaging
  • Tags attached and no damage
  • Original receipt or order number

Non-Returnable Items

  • Final sale items
  • Intimate apparel
  • Damaged or used items

Return Process

  1. Contact us at returns@savx.com
  2. Receive return authorization
  3. Ship item back with tracking
  4. Refund processed within 5-7 days

Refund Method

Refunds issued to original payment method. Shipping costs non-refundable.

Exchange Policy

Exchanges available for different sizes/colors of same item, subject to availability.
